Q. Is space infinite??

+

A. There's a limit to how much of the universe we can see. The observable universe is finite. It extends 46 billion light years in every direction from us.

Q. Will space ever end??

+

A. Well scientists don't believe there's an end to space. However, we can only see a certain volume of all that's out there. Since the universe is 13.8 billion years old, light from a galaxy more than 13.8 billion light-years away hasn't had time to reach us yet.
